Welcome to Chongyi Li's Homepage

Hi！I am Chongyi Li (李重仪). I received my Ph.D. degree from School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University, Tianjin, China. I conducted the Ph.D. research as a Visiting Student with Research School of Engineering, Australian National University, Canberra, Australia. I was advised by Prof. Jichang Guo in TJU and Prof. Fatih Porikli (IEEE Fellow) in ANU. Now, I am a postdoctoral fellow with Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong. I work with Prof. Sam Kwong (IEEE Fellow) in CityU, Hong Kong.

Research Interests:

    image processing, computer vision, deep learning, particularly in the domains of

  • image and video restoration and enhancement, such as images and videos captured in adverse weather (hazy, foggy, sandy, dusty, rainy, snowy day) and special circumstances (underwater, weak illumination);
  • RGB-D data related applications, such as color image guided depth super-resolution reconstruction and RGB-D salient object detection;
